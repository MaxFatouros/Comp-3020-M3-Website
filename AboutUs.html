About Us

We add value to people's lives through seamless online food delivery and pickup. At SkipTheDishes, we know you deserve great delivery. Our world-class, unparalleled technology powers millions of orders every month to bring hungry Canadians the food they want, delivered. Skip is Canada's leading and largest food delivery network, with tens of thousands of restaurants coast-to-coast in hundreds of communities across Canada. Our headquarters are in the heart of the continent in Winnipeg, Manitoba. SkipTheDishes is proudly part of Just Eat, a leading global marketplace for online food delivery headquartered in the UK.
